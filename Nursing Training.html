Course Modules

Comprehensive curriculum designed to provide you with essential healthcare knowledge and skills

Anatomy & Physiology

Understand the human body structure and functions to provide better patient care.

System Disorders & Management

Learn to identify and manage various system disorders in clinical settings.

Basic Pharmacology

Gain knowledge of medications, their effects, and safe administration practices.

Clinical Nursing Skills

Develop hands-on skills for patient care in various healthcare settings.

First Aid

Learn emergency response techniques to provide immediate care when needed.

3 Months Hospital Secondments

Gain practical experience through hospital placements and real-world training.

Drug Abuse Campaign

Participate in awareness and prevention initiatives to combat substance abuse in communities.
