Overview of Mission Charitable Trust

Our journey from a local initiative to a nationwide impact

Our Foundation

Mission Charitable Trust was founded in 2019 in Nyabira under Zvimba District in Mashonaland West Province. The Trust is headquartered in Nyabira, from where we coordinate our nationwide operations.

What began as a local initiative to address healthcare disparities has grown into a comprehensive organization serving thousands annually across multiple provinces.

  • Headquarters: Nyabira, Zvimba District
  • Founded: 2019
  • Provinces Served: 6 provinces across Zimbabwe
  • Communities Impacted: 45+ and growing

    Provinces We Serve

    Since its inception, Mission Charitable Trust has been operating in 6 provinces across Zimbabwe

    Mashonaland East

    District: Wedza District

    Our programs in Wedza District focus on mobile health units and health education, serving remote communities with limited healthcare access.

    Mashonaland West

    District: Zvimba District

    Our home province where we began our journey. We operate comprehensive healthcare programs including our headquarters in Nyabira.

    Mashonaland Central

    District: Mazowe District

    In Mazowe District, we focus on maternal and child health programs, providing essential care to families in remote areas.

    Masvingo

    District: ZAKA District

    Our operations in Zaka District include mobile health units, health screenings, and our nursing assistant training program.

    Midlands

    District: Gokwe North District

    In Gokwe North, we focus on combating infectious diseases and providing health education to remote communities.

    Manicaland

    District: Makoni

    Our programs in Makoni District include comprehensive healthcare services and community health worker training.

    Expansion Plans

    Growing our impact to reach more communities in need

    As part of our commitment to serving underserved communities across Zimbabwe, Mission Charitable Trust is planning to expand its operations to Matabeleland North Province, specifically targeting Binga District.

    Forthcoming Expansion

    Our needs assessment has identified significant healthcare challenges in Binga District, where communities face limited access to medical facilities and trained healthcare professionals.

    We plan to implement our proven model of mobile health units and community health worker training to address these challenges, with a focus on:

    • Mobile health clinics serving remote villages
    • Training local community health workers
    • Maternal and child health programs
    • Clean water and sanitation initiatives

    This expansion represents our commitment to ensuring all Zimbabweans have access to quality healthcare, regardless of their location or socioeconomic status.
